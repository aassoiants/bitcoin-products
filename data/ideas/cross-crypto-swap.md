| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| slug | cross-crypto-swap |
| idea | Swap bitcoin for another cryptocurrency peer-to-peer without a centralized exchange. |
| card_body | Bitcoin holders swapping sats for altcoins or stablecoins choose between centralized exchanges and imperfect decentralized options. The primitives work pair by pair. Build the peer-to-peer layer that covers arbitrary pairs. |
| body | Bitcoin holders swapping sats for altcoins or stablecoins choose between centralized exchanges and imperfect decentralized options. [THORChain](https://thorchain.org) and [Maya](https://mayaprotocol.com) route through shared liquidity pools: non-custodial but not P2P, and governance can freeze funds. [Bisq](https://bisq.network) matches counterparties directly via multisig escrow, not atomic. [Eigenwallet](https://eigenwallet.org) is a P2P atomic swap for BTC ↔ XMR only; scriptless-script doesn't generalize to other altcoins. The primitives work pair-by-pair. The P2P layer across arbitrary pairs doesn't exist. |
