## p2p-marketplace

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| slug | p2p-marketplace |
| idea | Run a peer-to-peer bitcoin marketplace without a centralized merchant account. |
| card_body | A bitcoin marketplace needs listings, reputation, escrow, and decentralized hosting. Past attempts went centralized and got shut down, or ran out of funding. The primitives exist today as separate pieces. Assemble them into the marketplace nobody can shut down. |
| body | Silk Road ran the first bitcoin marketplace at scale, with thousands of sellers, search, reviews, and escrow. It was centralized, and the FBI shut it down. [OpenBazaar](https://openbazaar.org) tried to port that shape without a central operator and ran out of funding. [Plebeian Market](https://plebeian.market) ships a self-hostable Nostr marketplace with Lightning checkout, but no escrow or reputation layer. [Scrow](https://github.com/storopoli/scrow) provides non-custodial multisig escrow on Nostr as a primitive. The pieces exist. Add your angle to a marketplace that resists shutdown. |
