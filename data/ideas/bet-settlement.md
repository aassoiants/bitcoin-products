| body | Friends settle bets on sports, elections, and weather every day. In bitcoin, that means trusting a sportsbook with custody or building your own DLC setup. [Thunderpick](https://thunderpick.io/) and its peers take custody. [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/) proved non-custodial prediction markets work at scale, but on Polygon with USDC, not bitcoin. [DLC Markets](https://dlcmarkets.com/) and [Lava](https://lava.xyz/) ship Discreet Log Contracts for institutional derivatives and loans. [dlcdevkit](https://github.com/bennyhodl/dlcdevkit) is the dev toolkit. Atomic Finance shipped a consumer DLC app, since wound down. [Son of Liberty](https://benschroth.com/blog/dlcdevkit-beyond/) wraps dlcdevkit into a friend-bet flow for Umbrel and Start9 nodes. The bitcoin rails exist. There's room for a consumer-friendly take. |
