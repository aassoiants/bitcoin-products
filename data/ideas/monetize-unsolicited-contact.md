| card_body | Senders blast DMs and emails for free while recipients eat the noise. Earn.com proved sender-pays-recipient works at scale, then got acquired and shut down. The rails exist across Lightning and Nostr. Build the cross-channel consumer gate. |
| body | Earn.com validated sender-pays-recipient at scale in 2015-2019 (hundreds of thousands of users, millions in revenue) before Coinbase acquired and shut it down. Nothing has filled the gap since. [LinkedIn InMail](https://linkedin.com) charges senders but keeps the fee; recipients get nothing. [Tanstaafl](https://tanstaafl.email) ships Lightning-gated email today, but senders need a Lightning wallet. Nostr's [NIP-57 Zaps](https://nips.nostr.com/57) give clients the payment rail; no major client ships a pay-to-DM toggle. The rails exist. The cross-channel consumer gate doesn't. |
