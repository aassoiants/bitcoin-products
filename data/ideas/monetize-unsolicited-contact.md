| idea | Monetize unsolicited contact to invert the spam economy. |
| card_body | Senders blast DMs and emails for free while recipients eat the noise. Earn.com proved sender-pays-recipient works at scale, then got acquired and shut down. The rails exist across Lightning and Nostr. Build the cross-channel consumer gate. |
| body | Earn.com validated sender-pays-recipient at scale (hundreds of thousands of users, millions in revenue) before Coinbase acquired and shut it down. [LinkedIn InMail](https://linkedin.com) charges senders but keeps the fee; recipients get nothing. [Tanstaafl](https://tanstaafl.email) ships Lightning-gated email, but senders need a Lightning wallet. Nostr's [NIP-57 Zaps](https://nips.nostr.com/57) give clients the payment rail; pay-to-DM toggles haven't broken into major clients. The rails exist. Plenty of room for new takes. |
