### Winners

- 1st: OPIDoki (bitcoin oracle for bets/contracts, Team "Party People": Chris DeRose + Arian Amador, site opidoki.com)
- 2nd: Seller.Trade (BitPay P2P shopping, Team "Frostwire": Angel Leon + Alden, [writeup](https://frostwire.wordpress.com/2015/01/12/frostwire-team-wins-2nd-place-at-the-miami-bitcoin-hackathon/))

### Notes

Jan 2015, 28-hour event, The LAB Miami; 1st annual; ~100 programmers + 70 observers; ~20 teams formed; organizers Doug Carrillo + Andrew Barnard (BitStop) + Peter Nova; prizes $10K / $3.5K / $1K in BTC; 1st: OPIDoki (bitcoin oracle for bets/contracts, Team "Party People": Chris DeRose + Arian Amador, site opidoki.com); 2nd: Seller.Trade (BitPay P2P shopping, Team "Frostwire": Angel Leon + Alden, [writeup](https://frostwire.wordpress.com/2015/01/12/frostwire-team-wins-2nd-place-at-the-miami-bitcoin-hackathon/)); 3rd: BitJuke (bitcoin jukebox, Team "AudioBits": Xavier Banegas + Alina Lebron + Nelson Milian + Willie Avendano + Derek Miller); other hack themes surfaced: bitcoin marketplaces, music platforms, smart contracts, gaming, bitcoin inheritance; no gallery, full winner details recovered from Wayback 2026-04-17
