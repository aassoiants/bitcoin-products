### Notes

Sept 2015, NYC (2-day event, preceded Consensus 2015 conference Sept 10); sponsors: Braintree, Chain, Coinbase, KeepKey; 15 teams, 10 on financial infrastructure + 5 on financial inclusion; $5K prize; winner: Team 15 blockchain insurance receipts Android app branded for USAA, anchored via Tierion's existing blockchain-receipts service + Google Sheets; team led by Wayne Vaughan (Tierion CEO) with Thulasi Nambiar (Citi), Deepak Atal (NYU CS), Dom Steil (Apttus), Emily Faber (Coin Cafe); judges incl. Adam Ludwin (Chain), Elizabeth Stark (Yale Law, now Lightning Labs), Nick Tomaino (Coinbase), Darin Stanchfield (KeepKey); bitcoin + Chain API focus but multi-chain eligible
